Confectionarium

Confectionarium is an online community for bakers and sweet-makers, both professional and enthusiast. It is a member-based platform where users can share recipes and techniques, show off creations, and seek advice. Their image is inviting, social, and delicious.

Icing Design System

Icing is a mixture of sugar with liquid or butter, typically flavored and colored, and used as a coating for cakes or cookies. We wanted to build a community for you guys to show off your talents and express yourself through your baked creations! At Confectionarium, we provide the platform for you to showcase your icing! Just as bakers use icing to decorate their creations, we wanted to design our platform to have a suitable look and feel to some of the sweetest baked creations.

Design Identity

Our designs can be summarized by a few words : fruity, delicious, appealing, dainty, indulgent
